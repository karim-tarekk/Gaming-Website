Grand Theft Auto V

.:Information:.

Grand Theft Auto V is a video game type open world Action-adventures straighten the first person perspective and third-person shooter developed by Rockstar North and published Rockstar Games . It was released on September 17, 2013 for Xbox 360 and Playstation 3 . Its release date for Microsoft Windows was announced on April 14, 2015. game is the fifteenth edition in the series. Grand Theft Auto 5 was one of the most anticipated games, being one of the last huge games to be released exclusively for the seventh generation of video games platforms . Grand Theft Auto 5 plays first and third person perspectives on PlayStation 4 , Xbox One, and Microsoft Windows , plays third-person perspective on PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 and combines driving and action elements in an open world, allowing players to interact with the world however they want. The events of the game take place in the fictional state of San Andreas, built on southern California , ensuring that the player roams freely around the countryside and the city of Los Santos, built on Los Angeles . The development of the story is told through three characters that are controlled and the player switches between them, namely: Michael, Trevor and Franklin. The story follows the efforts of the three bank robbers for wealth. There is in the game developed team play, Which allows up to sixteen players to participate in cooperative and competitive play. Game development began shortly after the release of Grand Theft Auto 4 , with an emphasis on improving the series' core mechanics by allowing the player to control three characters. Upon its release, the game received very positive reviews from critics who praised the story, its presentation, and the open world. The commercial success of the game broke records with revenues of 800 million US dollars during the first 24 hours of its release, or about 13 million copies. won the game award year game of many publications games.
